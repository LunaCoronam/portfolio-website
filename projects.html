Projects

Warden's Will

Multiplayer third-person action roguelite built around synergy-driven builds, high-mobility combat, and escalating intensity.

Core Design & 3Cs

Calculated and defined core combat pillars such as TTKs, engagement ranges, and the DPS/Burst/Range-AoE trifecta.

Weapon & Ability Designs

Calculated and tuned breakpoints for 14 weapons—each featuring unique primary and secondary fires—and 16 abilities, reinforcing clear playstyle identities and impactful choices.

Augment System Design

Developed a 140+ Augment System with layered in-run progression, synergistic interactions, branching upgrade paths, and stat-driven builds to reinforce varied playstyles.

Enemy & Difficulty Design

Built a roster of 18 enemies and 5 bosses, alongside a procedural difficulty director that selects complementary squads to pressure different player skills over time.

Meta Progression

Built meta-progression frameworks—player unlocks, long-term goals, and meta-currency economies—to drive sustained engagement.

Your Design

An expressive home-decoration game centered on visual creativity, friendly competition, a player-driven economy, and playful mini-games.

Home decoration and competition

Core gameplay revolves around decorating a room and submitting it to compete against other players in voting-based matches.

Daily Challenges, Player-Driven Market & Rewards

Built a procedural weekly challenge generator that delivers fresh tasks and soft-currency rewards for decoration purchases. Competitive play grants hard currency, which players can spend on high-grade items for trading or creating more advanced designs.

Simulation Mini-games

Designed 12+ hands-on simulation mini-games, ranging from kintsugi vase repair to fixing window panes.

Slime Farm

A casual mobile game where players catch slimes with a vacuum, refine slimes into gold, and complete fun challenges to expand their island.

Shops, Upgrades & Challenges

Player progression was provided with unlockable islands, upgradable vacuum parts and more efficient slime refineries.
