Projects

Warden's Will

A 3rd-person action roguelite centered around synergy-driven builds, fast-paced mobility, and evolving combat intensity.

Core Design & 3Cs

Calculated and established core pillars like Time to Kills (TTKs), engagement ranges and the combat trifecta (DPS, Burst, Range/AoE).

Weapon & Ability Designs

Calculated precise breakpoints for weapons and abilities to create different playstyles and make the player choices distinct from each other.

Augment System Design

Designed and refined the entire Augment System with in-run progression economy, allowing players to gain in-run strength through synergies, branching upgrades, and core stat boosts with varied playstyles.

Enemy & Difficulty Design

Designed all non-boss enemies and created a procedural dynamic difficulty system which called squads of complementary groups from the whole table to ensure different player strengths were tested over time.

Meta Progression

Designed meta progression in terms of player unlocks and meta currency economies to ensure long term player engagement.

Your Design

An expressive home decoration game focused on visual creativity, competition, player-driven economy and playful mini-games.

Home decoration and competition

Core gameplay of decorating and submitting the decoration to compete with other players in a voting match.

Economy design with daily challenges, player driven economy, competitions and rewards.

Created a procedural, weekly level design system to auto generate challenges which then rewards players with soft currency that can be spent on decorations. Players can then earn hard currencies through competing in these challenges and buying higher grade items to trade with others or use in new designs.

Simulation Mini-games

Created more than 12 simulation mini-games, ranging from kintsugi on a broken vase to window pane repair.
